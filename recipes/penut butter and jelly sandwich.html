How to make Peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Description

This easy to do recipe is widly regarded as one of the greatest type of sandwiches of all time. The saltness of the peanut butter is matched perfectly with the sweetness of the jelly. The paring is considered so perfect that people use peanut butter and jelly to describe other pairings and how perfect they are. The simple sandwich only takes 3 steps but is a healthy source of protien and nutrients

Ingredients

These are the very simple ingredients to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich...

  • jar of peanut butter
  • jar of jelly
  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 1 knife and 1 plate

Steps

These are the steps to make this sandwich...

  1. First toast your slices of bread till toasted
  2. Next after its toasted use the knife and spread the peanut butter on one of the toasts
  3. After you spread the peanut butter, you spread the jelly on the other toast
  4. After both toasts have spreasd you put two and two together
  5. Enjoy the sandwich, and make arond 5 more while your at it
